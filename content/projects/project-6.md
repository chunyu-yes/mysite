This line of work evaluates tea consumption as a potential causal exposure for multiple chronic diseases.

- Objective: Test whether tea consumption has causal effects on cardiovascular disease, diabetes, and cancer.
- Data: China Kadoorie Biobank, UK Biobank, and Biobank Japan.
- Methods: GWAS, instrument validation, one-sample and two-sample Mendelian randomization, and linearity testing.
- Status: Completed; selected publications available.

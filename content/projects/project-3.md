This project links sleep-disordered breathing and sleep-stage disruption to proteomic aging signals using high-dimensional SomaScan data.

- Objective: Quantify how AHI, ODI, oxygen desaturation, and REM/NREM architecture map onto proteomic age acceleration.
- Data: Two waves of sleep measures integrated with SomaScan proteomics.
- Methods: Proteomic clock construction, feature engineering, enrichment analysis, and mixed-effects modeling.
- Status: Active.

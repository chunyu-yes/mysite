This work develops stronger methods for quantifying famine intensity and evaluating dose-response relationships in Chinese famine studies.

- Objective: Improve exposure measurement for prenatal famine analyses and downstream health studies.
- Data: Census, mortality, and cohort-based population data.
- Methods: Cohort shrinkage modeling, excess mortality estimation, and validation across empirical studies.
- Status: Completed; methods presented at SER 2022.

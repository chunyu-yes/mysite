This project uses spatial transcriptomics to characterize microregional organization and signaling patterns in the human habenula.

- Objective: Map functional spatial domains and infer signaling networks across microregions.
- Data: Visium HD spatial transcriptomics with scRNA-seq integration.
- Methods: Spatial clustering, representation learning, batch correction, and graph-based cell-cell communication modeling.
- Status: Active.

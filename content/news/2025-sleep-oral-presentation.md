Accepted for an oral presentation at SLEEP 2025 in Seattle on "Circadian Rest-Activity Rhythms and Epigenetic Age Acceleration in Older Adults."

The project examines whether actigraphy-derived rhythm disruption is associated with accelerated epigenetic aging in older adults.

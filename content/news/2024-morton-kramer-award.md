Received The Morton Kramer Fund Award at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health in 2024.

This award recognizes outstanding doctoral work in the Department of Mental Health, with particular emphasis on excellence in applying biostatistical and epidemiological methods to research on the epidemiology and prevention of mental disorders.

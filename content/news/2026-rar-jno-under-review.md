The manuscript on circadian rest-activity rhythms and epigenetic age acceleration in older adults is currently under review at JAMA Network Open.

The study evaluates whether actigraphy-derived circadian rhythm features are associated with DNA methylation-based age acceleration and whether those relationships vary by age, sex, and BMI.

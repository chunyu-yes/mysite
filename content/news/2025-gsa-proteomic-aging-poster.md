Presented the poster "Associations Between Objective Sleep Measures and Proteomic Aging: The ARIC Study" at the 2025 Gerontological Society of America meeting in Boston.

This work highlights how sleep phenotypes can be used as informative exposures in multi-omics studies of biological aging.
